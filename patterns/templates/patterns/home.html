Welcome to our remote experiment.

Our experiment is testing what representations (Bubble or Text) is more useful for representing graphical data.

Your objective is to navigate to the Bubble/Text tabs to complete a series of trials. There will be 9 trials for each representation and your goal is to click on the largest bubble/numbered value.

Please register a user account by navigating to the registration tab or clicking the link bellow.
